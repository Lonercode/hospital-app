A Century of Care

We have made it our century old mission to provide the best healthcare Services at affordable rates. Our history began in 1924 when William Bach our founder opened the doors of the institution to the community.
Over the years, allCare has expanded its facilities and expertise. Today we stand as a leading healthcare institution equipped with cutting-edge technology and a team of compassionate professionals who are committed to improving lives everyday.

Our Mission

To enhance the health and well-being of our patients and the communities we serve.

Our Services

A diverse range of services ranging from emergency trauma and care to rehabilitation and physical therapy.

Our Team

Our team consists of dedicated healthcare professionals, including board-certified physicians, nurses and supoort staff.

Community Engagement

We are proud to be actively involved in our community through health education programs, screenings and partnerships with local and international organizations.
